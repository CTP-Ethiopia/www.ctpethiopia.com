Connecting students to employers in Ethiopia

Before you begin exploring this database, ﻿please follow this link ﻿to learn about the "Careers in Ethiopia" project.

Throughout our time in Ethiopia, we have had the honor to meet representatives  of companies deemed to be influential leaders in their respective industries in Ethiopia. Do you want to speak to professionals for career advice, or discuss career or internship opportunities in their organizations? Have you found it difficult to reach organizations? We are here to help! Browse through the database of the following companies in Ethiopia that are making a difference. If you would like to make a connection, click on the “Connect” button right next to the company. This web application will forward your information to the firm. 

We would love to hear your opinions. Email us at: info.ctpethiopia@gmail.com

Veritas Consulting, PLC is a market advisory firm focused on the private and public sectors of Ethiopia. Veritas’ Managing Partners possess extensive expertise in delivering consulting, financial and legal services to multi-nationals and leading local companies, international development agencies, and government institutions. Our specific services include development and private sector consulting services with an emphasis on: market entry and advisory; investment regulations and reform; economic policy formulation; corporate and public finance; transaction services; tax advisory and reform; and legal advising.
